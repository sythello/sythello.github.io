Introduction

I'm currently a Full-time Machine Learning Engineer at Tiktok, San Jose. My current work focus is on video recommendation.

I previously received my PhD at UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Ndapa Nakashole. During my PhD, my research areas are Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. My main research focus is on natural language interfaces, i.e. to allow users to use systems via natural language instructions, so as to reduce the learning overhead or expertise requirement. I'm interested in the ways to construct and utilize structured data for natural language interfaces, including tables, databases (DB), knowledge bases (KB), etc. I'm interested in many relevant NLP tasks, including task-oriented dialogs, text-to-SQL parsing, text generation and knowledge base construction.

Before my PhD, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Peking University.
